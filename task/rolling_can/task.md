### **Q3 PROJECT 4 - CIRCULAR SHAPE MOVEMENT**

#### **Instructions**

In this assignment, you will detect a moving circular shape from a video. It must be a video taken by you, and you ARE NOT allowed to share videos. You must use a soda can or a can of that shape.

You will take a video of the can and using image overlay, you must overlay the circumference of the can with a circular line moving following the shape of the base.

You must also find the center point of the circle while moving.

THE CIRCLE MUST BE THE OUTER RING OF THE BOTTOM OF THE CAN. THE CIRCLE MUST NOT WOBBLE AND IT MUST NOT FLICKER IN AND OUT. YOU ARE NOT ALLOWED TO MAKE ANY MARKS ON THE CAN OR ALTER THE APPEARANCE OF THE CAN IN ANY WAY.

---

#### **Assessment**

You will be assessed on the following:

* **Image quality:** 10 points
* **Image Overlay:** 25 points
* **Image overlay accuracy:** 25 points
* **Center point detection:** 25 points
* **Code:** 15 points (5 points for proper comments and documentation, 5 points for proper format, 5 points for code layout and use of pseudo code)

you can qualitatively inspect the output video to improve your solution





#### **output format**

output to a csv file with the following
Frame_Index,X,Y,Radius
